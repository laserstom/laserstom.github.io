Services

LaserStom offers a full array of
dental services to help you maintain healthy teeth.

  • Cosmetic Dentistry

    It is our belief that everyone deserves to look and feel their best! We believe this because a healthy and beautiful smile contributes to confidence and self esteem.

  • Laser Dentistry

    Lasers can reduce symptoms and healing times associated with traditional therapies, reduce the amount of bacteria in both diseased gum tissue and in tooth cavities, and control bleeding during surgery.

  • Dentures

    If you’ve lost all of your natural teeth, whether from gum disease, tooth decay or injury, replacing missing teeth will benefit your appearance and your health.

  • Teeth Whitening

    Teeth whitening can remove years off of the age of your smile. Your teeth will be brighter and shine brighter than they have in decades

  • Gum Treatment

    Gum disease or Periodontal Disease is the most common cause of tooth loss. Should gum problems arise, you may need periodontal therapy to restore diseased tissues to health.

  • Dental Implants

    Dental implants combine both functionality and beautiful aesthetics to give patients smiles that they love.
